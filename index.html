Papyrus is a natural handwriting note-taking app for Android that you can use just like paper, but with the flexibility and advantages of modern technology.

Why we matter!

Active Pen Technology

  • Pressure sensitive for more natural writing
  • Button support to enable faster tooling options
  • Quicker more intuitive gesturing

Innovative Note Taking

  • Organize notes within digital notebooks
  • Export notes to PDF for printing, archiving, or sharing
  • Share notes with friends and colleagues via email, Evernote, and other services

Key Features

  • Pen strokes are vector graphics
  • Undo/redo strokes
  • Cut, copy, and paste strokes between notes
  • Select and move strokes
  • Two finger scroll

Digital Collection!

Sort different notes into various digital notebooks in addition to starring important notes to be kept at the top - readily available.

Visual Notebook!

Write Forever!
