Natural handwriting, beyond paper.

Papyrus is a natural handwriting note-taking app for Android that you use just like paper, but with the flexibility and advantages of modern technology. With Papyrus, you can go beyond paper!

Papyrus received Category Honorable Mention in the Samsung Galaxy Note S Pen App Challenge!

What does it do?

Active Pen Technology

  • Pressure sensitive for more natural writing
  • Button support to enable faster tooling options
  • Quick, intuitive gestures for tasks and tools
  • Support includes:
    • HTC Flyer, Jetstream, and EVO View 4G (HTC Scribe Pen)
    • Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet
    • Samsung Galaxy Note and Galaxy Note 10.1 (S Pen)

Innovative Note Taking

  • Organize notes within digital notebooks
  • Export notes to PDF for printing, archiving, or sharing
  • Share notes with friends and colleagues via email, Evernote, and other services
  • Choose between multiple paper types and sizes

Advanced Features

  • Pen strokes are vector graphics
  • Undo/redo strokes
  • Cut, copy, and paste strokes between notes
  • Select and move strokes
  • Two finger scroll
  • Two finger pinch-to-zoom
  • Two double tap to zoom to 100%
