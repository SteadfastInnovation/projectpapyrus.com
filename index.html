Natural handwriting, beyond paper.

Papyrus is a natural handwriting note-taking app for Android that you use just like paper, but with the flexibility and advantages of modern technology. With Papyrus, you can go beyond paper!

What does it do?

Active Pen Technology

  • Pressure sensitive for more natural writing
  • Button support to enable faster tooling options
  • Quick, intuitive gestures for tasks and tools
  • Supports the HTC Flyer, HTC Jetstream, HTC EVO View 4G (HTC Scribe Pen) and Lenovo ThinkPad Tablet

Innovative Note Taking

  • Organize notes within digital notebooks
  • Export notes to PDF for printing, archiving, or sharing
  • Share notes with friends and colleagues via email, Evernote, and other services

More Awesome Features

  • Pen strokes are vector graphics
  • Undo/redo strokes
  • Cut, copy, and paste strokes between notes
  • Select and move strokes
  • Two finger scroll

See for yourself!

Digital Collection!

Sort different notes into various digital notebooks in addition to starring important notes to be kept at the top - readily available.

See thumbnails of the notes inside each of your notebooks.

Tool Picker

Choose between pen, selection, and eraser tools.

Select individual thickness for pen and eraser tools.

Color Picker

Choose your pen color!

Recent colors saves your last eight colors.

Scrolling and Sharing

Use two fingers to scroll/pan a page.

Export notes to PDF and share them with others!

Cut, Copy, Paste!

Select strokes with the selection tool.
Cut, copy, paste, and duplicate selected strokes!
Move strokes within a page, note, or even to other notes!

Active Pen Gestures!

Perform gestures for common tasks on active pen devices!

Hold the side but and swipe up/down/left/right to perform quick tasks.

Single Finger Erase!

Erase strokes with a single finger on active pen devices!

Active Pen Selection

Use the selection tool with your active pen simply by holding the side button and drawing a "lasso" around strokes!

Hold the pen side button and tap to access cut/copy/paste.
